KLE CBA presents to you

on 20th and 21st March 2020


Aavishkar is the mega event hosted by KLE CBA. It is a national level management fest organized for all the BBA colleges across India. It is purely a management fest where in students belonging to commerce and allied fields such as Human resource, Finance, and Marketing participate in the event that involves skills similar to those which they face in the corporate world. It is a great platform for the students where they will improve their creative and management skills which will help them to be prepared for their career.

Cash prize of Rs. 25000 for the GC winning team.
